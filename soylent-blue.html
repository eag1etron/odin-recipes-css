Soylent Blue

Description

Blueberries added to Soylent Green.

Ingredients

  • Soylent Green
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a big bowl.
  2. Pour mixture into large pan.
  3. Let sit overnight, it will harden.
  4. Cut into wafers to serve.
  5. Remix it with water to make a smoothie.
