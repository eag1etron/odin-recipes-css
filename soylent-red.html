Soylent Red

Description

Red secret ingredient added to Soylent Green.

Ingredients

  • Soylent Green
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Secret ingredient that turns soylent red

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a big bowl.
  2. Pour mixture into large baking pan and bake @ 350 for 1 hour..
  3. Let sit overnight, it will harden.
  4. Cut into wafers and serve.
  5. Remix it with water to make a smoothie.
