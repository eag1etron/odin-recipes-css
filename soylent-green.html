Soylent Green

Description

The original soylent. (what is that?)

Ingredients

  • Soylent
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Kale

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a big bowl.
  2. Get all the clumps out.
  3. Pour mixture into large baking pan.
  4. Let sit overnight, it will harden.
  5. Cut into wafers and serve the people.
  6. Remix it with water to make a smoothie.
